An SIA licence is required to work legally in many UK security jobs including door supervision, CCTV and guarding roles.
You can enroll in an approved training course and complete the required certification process.
Most SIA training courses can be completed within 2–3 weeks depending on the course type.
Prices vary depending on the course and training provider.
After completing training, you can apply online through the official SIA website.
It means the qualification is officially approved and recognized by the Security Industry Authority.
